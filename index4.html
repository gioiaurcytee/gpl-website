About the Lounge

A modern talk show space rooted in women’s health and real life conversation.

Hosted by Gioi AurcyTeē, Gioi’s Perinatal Lounge brings together education, lived experience, interviews, and meaningful dialogue across the maternal and family journey.

This brand creates room for women and families to engage with honest conversations around wellness, pregnancy, postpartum, infant care, support systems, and the larger picture of womanhood.

What you’ll find here

  • Maternal wellness education
  • Talk show interviews and guest features
  • Pregnancy and postpartum conversations
  • Infant care and parenting support
  • Women’s health dialogue across seasons of life
