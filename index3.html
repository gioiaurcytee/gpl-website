About the Lounge

A grounded, elegant space for education and real discussion.

Hosted by Gioi AurcyTeē, Gioi’s Perinatal Lounge brings together interviews, educational conversations, lived experiences, and community-centered dialogue.

This platform highlights the voices, stories, and information that matter to women and families, with a special focus on maternal wellness, postpartum support, infant care, and the broader journey of womanhood.

What this brand represents

  • Maternal wellness education
  • Talk show interviews and guest features
  • Women’s health conversations
  • Pregnancy and postpartum support
  • Community events and audience engagement
